About

This application allows you to manage your comic book collection. You can add comic books to your collection, rate them, complete information, etc. You can also add comic books to your wishlist if you don't own them yet!

Book searches are done using the ISBN number: this is the book's barcode.

You can search a book by giving its ISBN, or detect it from a photo uploaded from your computer or captured from your webcam!

Have fun !
